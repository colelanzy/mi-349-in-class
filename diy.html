DIY Projects / Woodworking

Over the last year or so, I have loved working on small projects. Pictured on the right is a corn hole board that I built this summer '24. Could I have went to the store and just bought a new set of corn hole boards? Of course, but what's the fun in that ? Instead I spent hours throughout several days working on them, buying materials, cutting wood, sanding, putting it all together and finally staining them. There is something about making something yourself that makes it much more valuable to you. I am looking forwards to whatever my next project will be.
